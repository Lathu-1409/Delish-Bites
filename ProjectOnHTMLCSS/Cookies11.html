The secret to the best red velvet cookies is buttermilk and baking soda! These cookies are soft and reddish which attracts the Red Lovers!!

    Ingredients:
  • 1 ounce cocoa powder
  • 12 ounces all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 7 ounces unsalted butter (room temperature)
